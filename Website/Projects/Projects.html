Department Store Inventory

A department store utilizes a user menu to manage information about 75 items in its stock. The data is read from a text file during the initial loading process, and hash techniques are employed to efficiently handle record access, ensuring proper collision resolution. The query functionality allows users to retrieve details such as available units, base price, and expiration date using the product identifier.

Olympics (PODIUM)

Olympics (PODIUM)

Three binary search trees, GOLD, SILVER, and BRONZE, store Olympic medalists based on their results in the competitions (gold, silver, or bronze). Each medal has a point value (10, 5, and 2 respectively). Available information includes the participant's name and nationality. The trees are sorted alphabetically by the athlete's name.
